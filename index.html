About Us

Topzonke is a Johannesburg-based engineering company specializing in 3D printing, waste management, and telecommunications. We partner with OEMs and global experts to deliver sustainable solutions.

Our Approach:

Engineering-first mindset: Every project is built on technical excellence. Innovation-driven: Applying 3D and 4D printing, data farming, and smart technologies. Partnerships with OEMs: Collaborating with original equipment manufacturers to deliver scalable solutions. Sustainability focus: Waste-to-resource projects and eco-friendly materials.
